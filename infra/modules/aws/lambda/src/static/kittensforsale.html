~~~ABOUT PRICE~~~

We do NOT sell kittens with breeding rights.
We do NOT sell kittens overseas, we will occasionally trade with an overseas breeder for outcross lines.

Kittens are sold as PETS ONLY, not to be shown or bred.
Kittens may be show quality, show potential, or may have a small fault which would disqualify them from showing.
If you are wanting to Show a kitten, then we would help you pick out a kitten that may do well at a show.
Things that may make a kitten not suitable for show could be, pattern not bright and clear, color not rich enough,
earset or profile off, short tail, tail kink (which can show up as late as 5 months old),
those awesome pouty pronounced whisker pads (not desired for show),or potential underbite etc.
Although these things would not be good for show, they have no bearing on a Super Pet!

Many people prefer the darker tabbys, as they look a bit wilder. They may prefer a higher set ear,
or a smoke that isn't clearly breaking, although not good for show they have that AWESOME FERAL look!
A show kitten would be more moderate in type than we prefer at CongoCoons!
Although we prefer a wilder more feral type, we are currently breeding some fantastic show quality browns.
Sporting rich red and gold with brilliant clear patterns.
"Missing Link", "Primeval" and "Iron Maiden" are giving us some fantastic warm and boldly patterned offspring!


We have recently retired a number of adults, and are trying to not mix and dilute our colors.
We are working towards a strong Blue Silver, and a Warm Brown breeding program!
We have our new Odd Eyed Stud cat and will be working him into our program.
All kittens are born with blue eyes, which eventually turn green - green/gold or gold.
With solid white cats keeping a blue eye. We hope to have solid colored cats with odd eyes in the future!
Note: If kittens are pictured at 5 weeks or so they may still show a blueish eye, this will change.
Often the eye goes through a copper color stage, don't get attached : ) this will not stay.




Although we have had many Champion Cats, it has been years since we competed.
We have imported some Extreme Type cats recently, and will update our webpage shortly with their photos.
It is a constant work in progress to breed that SUPER CONGOCOON!

OUR BABY KITTENS ARE BORN IN THE NURSERY IN OUR HOME

Kittens are using a litter box by five weeks of age, and have playpens in the Kitten Room adjacent to our den once they begin to toddle around.
When you come to get your kitten, he will be freshly groomed, and taken to the Cub Cabin for pick up.
Kittens are not housed in the Cub Cabin, it is for pick-up only.

Once you have RESERVED a kitten, you can rest assured no strangers will be handling your baby!

Kittens are wormed at 3 and 5 weeks of age, and are given their first innoculation at 6 weeks of age. Kittens are vet checked at around 7 weeks of age.
We give a 1 year health guarantee (death due to congenital defect) and an 8 week FIP guarantee.
No Registration Papers provided on pet kittens.

There are usually a number of adult cats available for viewing in the cattey garden, so you can see what your kitten may develop into!
